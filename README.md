## How to Use

### Getting Started

1. **Add Your Income**
   - Go to "Financial Input" page
   - Add all your income sources
   - Specify amounts and frequencies (monthly, annual, etc.)

2. **Track Your Expenses**
   - Add your expenses with categories
   - Classify each expense as a "need" or "want"
   - Include existing savings contributions if applicable
   - The app will automatically calculate totals

3. **Enter Your Debts (Optional)**
   - Add any loans, credit cards, or other debts
   - Enter principal amount, interest rate, and monthly payment
   - View debt payoff strategies and projections

4. **Explore Savings Strategies**
   - Visit the "Savings Strategies" page
   - Compare popular budgeting methods
   - See how your actual spending compares to recommended allocations
   - Create a custom strategy that fits your lifestyle

5. **Analyze Your Finances**
   - Check the "Analytics" page for visual insights
   - View spending breakdowns by category
   - Understand your debt payoff timeline
   - Track your progress over time

6. **Plan Your Investments**
   - Use the "Investment Advice" calculator
   - Project how your savings can grow with compound interest
   - Experiment with different contribution amounts and time horizons
   - Visualize your path to financial goals

### Saving Your Data

**Important:** Your data is stored in your browser's session storage, which means it's only available in the current tab and will be lost when you close the browser.

To preserve your data:
1. Click the download icon in the header
2. Select "Export Data"
3. Save the JSON file to your computer
4. Later, use "Import Data" to restore your information

## Privacy & Data Security

### Your Data Stays Private

- **No Data Collection**: We don't collect, transmit, or store your personal financial data on any external servers
- **No Analytics**: No tracking, no cookies, no advertising
- **Browser-Only Storage**: All data stays in your browser's session storage
- **No Account Required**: No sign-up, no email, no personal information needed

### Data Storage

- Data is stored temporarily in your browser's session storage
- Automatically cleared when you close the browser tab
- You can manually clear data anytime using the "Clear Data" option
- Exported files are saved only on your device

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my financial data safe?
Yes! Your data never leaves your browser. It's stored locally in session storage and is not transmitted to any external servers.

### Do I need to create an account?
No! The app works completely without any registration or login.

### Will my data persist if I close the browser?
No. Session storage is cleared when you close the browser tab. Use the Export feature to save your data permanently.

### Can I use this on mobile?
Yes! The interface is responsive and works on mobile devices, though the experience is optimized for desktop.

### Which savings strategy should I use?
It depends on your personal situation. The 50/30/20 rule is a good starting point for most people, but you can experiment with different strategies or create your own.

### Are the investment calculations accurate?
The calculations use standard compound interest formulas, but they're simplified for educational purposes. Real investments involve fees, taxes, and market volatility not accounted for here.

### Can I trust this app with my financial planning?
This app is a learning tool, not a replacement for professional financial advice. Use it to explore concepts and understand your finances, but consult a financial advisor for important decisions.
